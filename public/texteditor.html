How We Count

Our counting methodology is transparent and customizable:

  • Words: Split text at whitespace (spaces, tabs, newlines) and count non-empty tokens. Hyphenated words like "state-of-the-art" are counted as one word when the option is enabled.
  • Characters: Total number of characters including spaces and punctuation.
  • Characters (no spaces): Total characters excluding all whitespace.
  • Sentences: Split text at periods, exclamation marks, and question marks. Consecutive punctuation is treated as a single sentence break.
  • Paragraphs: Split text at double line breaks (two or more consecutive newlines).
  • Reading Time: Calculated based on average reading speed of 200 words per minute.

You can customize the counting behavior using the options above. This ensures the counts match your specific needs and expectations.
